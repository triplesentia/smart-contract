What is a smart contract?


A smart contract is a computer protocol

designed to automate the execution

of terms and agreements within

decentralized systems such as blockchain.

In fact, it is a computer program that

monitors and ensures the fulfillment of

obligations in transactions.

What are its advantages?


Smart contracts allow you to control

the entire chain of product delivery:

from shipment by the manufacturer

to receipt by the end user.

All information about the movement of

goods is stored unchanged on the

blockchain,and smart contracts monitor

compliance with all delivery conditions.

Is it possible to use smart contracts
to conclude all types of contracts?


Yes, smart contracts can be used to conclude

any kind of contracts that can be expressed

through digital assets and automated processes.
